Felis Domesticus

This particular household animal is reserved and low-maintenance.

  • Scientific Name: Felis Catus
  • Average Length: 18 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years
  • Habitat:Typical Human House

Felis Domesticus are normally independent and require little attention. They loved to be scratched behind the ears and under the belly. They normally do not fare well with dogs. Beware of their eating habits, for if not monitored, these cats will develop diabetes and obesity.
